Parks

Aliyar Dam Resovoir Park

  • Large park offering gardens, playgrounds & picnic areas beside a dam with water & mountain views.
  • The park at the base of the dam and it is opened on all days from 09 am to 5 p.m in the evening.
  • Place:Coimbatore


    • Anna Tower Park


  • Anna Nagar Tower Park, officially known as Dr Visvesvaraya Tower Park, is an urban park in the suburb of Anna Nagar, Chennai. It is the tallest park tower in Chennai. The tower is open for public access from March 21st 2023. The park is also open.
  • Anna Nagar Tower Park is located near the chief roundabout of the neighbourhood (known as the Anna Nagar Roundana) and the Anna Nagar Ayyappan Temple. The park has two entrances. The main entrance is located on the 3rd Main Road next to the Tower Club. The second entrance lies near the Ayyappan Temple on the 6th Main Road.
  • Place:Chennai

    • Bryant Park

  • Recreation area featuring walking/cycling paths around a manmade lake with small boat rentals.
  • A park to relax after having a boating in the nearby Kodai lake. A medium sized park with lots of varieties of trees and beautiful flowers.
  • Place:kodaikanal


    • Semmozhi Poonga


  • Semmozhi Poonga, is a botanical garden in Chennai set up jointly by the Horticulture and Agricultural Engineering department of the Government of Tamil Nadu.
  • The garden features eight subgardens displaying flora of different varieties, such as a tree court, mural walk and bonsai, herbal and exotic gardens, in addition to an artificial duck pond. Incorporating elements of an Indian-Buddhist garden, the garden has a wide variety of indigenous species across 22 exclusive areas .
  • Place:Chennai

    • Yelagiri Nature Park

  • The nature park is very well maintained and it is studded with plenty of varieties of trees, well laid out walk ways, beautifully lighted fountain and so on.
  • This nature park is also known as the Government Herbal Farm. The park consists of bamboo houses as well as poly houses.
  • Place:Yelagiri
